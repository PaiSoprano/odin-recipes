Ziti

Description

A vegeterian version of a dish, made famous by The Sopranos. Serves four.

Ingredients

  • 300g De Cecco Penne
  • 400g Good Quality Tinned Tomatoes
  • 100ml Red Wine
  • 1 Onion
  • 50g Tomato Puree
  • 1 Carrot
  • 1 Chili
  • 2 Garlic Cloves
  • 200g Vegeterian Mince Meat
  • Olive Oil
  • 300g Cottage Cheese
  • 50g Grated Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Set oven to 175Â°C.
  2. With a pan on a medium heat add olive oil and cook garlic, chili and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add carrot and vegeterian mince to pan, meanwhile cook the pasta in a separate pan until 'al dente'.
  4. Once the vegetables and mince are softend, season to taste and add tinnned tomatoes, red wine, tomoato puree and reduce to a simmer.
  5. After the sauce has been cooking for 10 minutes, combined half the sauce and half the pasta in a caserole dish.
  6. Top the pasta with cottage cheese and spread all over with back of a spoon.
  7. Repeat previous two steps, adding the second layer of pasta and cottage cheese.
  8. Sprinkle over grated parmesan cheese.
  9. Bake in oven for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is golden brown and nicely melted.
