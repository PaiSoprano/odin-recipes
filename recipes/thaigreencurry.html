Thai Green Curry

Description

One of the classic Thai dishes.

Ingredients

  • 200g Chicken Breast, chopped
  • 1 Carrot, diced
  • 150g White Potatoes
  • 3 Spring Onions, sliced
  • 1 Tin Bamboo Shoots
  • 5 Red Chilis, sliced
  • 1 Garlic Clove, crushed
  • 1 Red Pepper, chopped
  • 400ml Coconut Milk
  • 2 tbs Thai Green Curry Paste
  • Bunch Fresh Corriander, chopped
  • 1 Lime
  • 1 tbs Fish Sauce
  • 1 tbs Vegeteable Oil
  • 300g White Rice

Steps

  1. In a large wok on a medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil with the curry paste.
  2. Once the oil and paste are well combined and fragrant, add the crushed garlic clove and chopped chicken.
  3. As the chicken becomes brown add all the other vegetables and cook down for a further 5 minutes.
  4. Once the vegetables are combined and softened, add the coconut milk, followed by the fish sauce, half the corriander and a squeeze half of the lime over.
  5. When the curry starts to bubble, reduce the heat to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes or until the sauce has slightly thickened. Meanwhile, cook the rice.
  6. Once the curry and rice have cooked plate up, serving with lime wedges and a sprinkle of the leftover corriander.
