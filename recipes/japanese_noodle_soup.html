Japanese Noodle Soup

Description

A delicious, winter warming Japanese dish.

Ingredients

  • 200g Chicken Breast, chopped
  • 1 Carrot, diced
  • 200g Ramen Noodles
  • 3 Spring Onions, sliced
  • Sesame Seeds
  • 2 Red Chilis
  • 1 Garlic Clove, crushed
  • 1 tsp Fresh Grated Ginger
  • 1.5 litres of Chicken Stock
  • 1 Bok Choi
  • 1 tbs Vegetable Oil
  • Dark Soy Sauce (for serving)

Steps

  1. Heat half the oil in a large pan and cook the chopped chicken.
  2. Once the chicken is golden brown, remove from the pan to a bowl lined with kitchen paper.
  3. Add the remaning oil, garlic, ginger and most (but not all) of the spring onion and gently cook.
  4. Once the ingredients in the pan are soft, add the carrot and bok choi and cook for a further few minutes.
  5. Now pour in the chicken stock and bring to the boil: reducing to a simmer after a few minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper. Meanwhile, cook the noodles to the packet instructions.
  6. Once the noodles are cooked add them to the pan along with the cooked chicken and simmer for 1 minute.
  7. Serve the soup in deep bowls, topping with the left over spring onions, sesame seeds and a splash or two of soy sauce.
